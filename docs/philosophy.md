# Philosophy

> *Leave clues, not paperwork.*

## Mission

Reduce the cognitive cost of administrative work.

People should spend their time doing their job, not reconstructing it afterwards.

Clew exists to help people capture work as it happens and reconstruct it later with confidence.

---

## Vision

Administrative work should begin with evidence, not memory.

A day's work consists of conversations, interruptions, decisions and actions. By the end of the day, those events compete with dozens of others for attention.

Clew provides a thread back through the day's work.

---

## Core Principle

**Capture work once. Generate everything else.**

The smallest useful record is usually sufficient.

One line written at the right moment is worth far more than a page reconstructed hours later.

---

## Design Principles

### Markdown first

Markdown is the source of truth.

Your notes remain yours, readable without Clew, today and twenty years from now.

---

### Local first

Clew runs locally by default.

Your notes stay where you choose to keep them.

Cloud services should be optional, never required.

---

### Humans first

Humans optimise for writing.

Computers optimise for reading.

Clew adapts to the way people naturally record work, not the other way around.

---

### Events, not documents

Clew records work events.

It does not become another document repository.

Evidence belongs in the systems that created it.

Clew simply links the work together.

---

### Augment memory

Clew assists recollection.

It does not replace judgement.

Every report remains the responsibility of the person who performed the work.

---

### Framework, not workflow

Clew describes work.

It does not dictate how organisations use that information.

Plugins and applications generate reports, exports and integrations.

---

## What Clew is not

Clew is not:

- a ticketing system
- a CRM
- a case management system
- a document management system
- a time tracking application
- an AI that writes reports on your behalf

Those systems already exist.

Clew helps bridge the gap between doing the work and recording the work.

---

## Success

Success is not measured by how much paperwork Clew generates.

Success is measured by how little effort is required to remember what happened.

If Clew saves someone twenty minutes every day by helping them reconstruct their work accurately, it has succeeded.

Everything else is an extension of that goal.

---

## Ethics

The human remains in control.

Clew makes suggestions.

Clew reconstructs timelines.

Clew generates drafts.

It never assumes responsibility for professional judgement.

---

## The Thread

In Greek mythology, Ariadne gave Theseus a clew of thread so he could find his way back out of the Labyrinth.

The modern word **clue** is derived from **clew**.

Clew exists for the same reason.

Not to walk the path for you.

Simply to leave enough thread that you can always find your way back.
